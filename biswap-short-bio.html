Short Biography

Biswapratap Singh Sahoo is currently working as a Device & Modem Standards Research Expert at Nokia. He has over 10+ years of experience in wireless communications and NextGen networks. Previously, he worked as a Lead Engineer at Samsung Research.

He pursued his Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering at the National Taiwan University (NTU) Taiwan. Before starting his Ph.D. at NTU, he briefly worked at the IIT Kanpur and the Council of Scientific & Industrial Research (CSIR-IMMT), Bhubaneswar.

He is the recipient of the 2021 Wireless Telecommunication Symposium Best Student Paper Award, and 2021 IEEE Computer Society Bio-Inspired Computing STC Outstanding Service Award. He has many IEEE international conferences, journals, transactions, and magazine publications to his credit. He is actively participating and contributing to 3GPP technology standardization on next-generation wireless communication technologies, particularly 5G-Advanced and 6G.

Hello. Is it me you're looking for?

It is my great pleasure to welcome you to my homepage. I am a Senior Researcher at Samsung Research India, Bangalore. I worked as a Ph.D. researcher in Electrical Engineering at National Taiwan University (NTU) Taiwan, researching on mmWave and 5G networks with Professor Hung-Yu Wei. I'm grateful to MediaTek Inc., Industrial Technology Research Institute (ITRI), and the Ministry of Science and Technology (MOST), Taiwan (R.O.C.), for their generous support for my research work.

Prior to starting my Ph.D. at NTU, I worked at the Indian Institute of Technology, Kanpur, and Council of Scientific & Industrial Research (CSIR-IMMT), Bhubaneswar, India. During Spring 2010, I worked at IIIT Bhubaneswar, India, as part of my master thesis. You can read more about me using the link here.

To this end, my mission is to utilize my passion in analyzing the implications of rapidly changing technology for socioeconomic inequities at large while serving as an example of the success that will shape the future of our society.

Research Interests

  • Emerging communications and networking technologies (5G/6G, mmWave, Edge/Fog)
  • Internet of Things including IIoT, UAV, V2X
  • Application of AI and ML in wireless
  • Bio-inspired computing & networking

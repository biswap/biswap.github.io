Hello. Is it me you're looking for?

It is my great pleasure to welcome you to my homepage. I'm a 3GPP Standards Researcher in the Device & Modem Research Group at Nokia Bangalore, India, also pursuing my Ph.D. degree at National Taiwan University (NTU) Taiwan. I'm grateful to MediaTek Inc., Industrial Technology Research Institute (ITRI), and the Ministry of Science and Technology (MOST), Taiwan (R.O.C.), for their generous support for my research work.

Previously, I was with the Advanced Modem Group at Samsung R&D India. Prior to that, I worked at the Indian Institute of Technology, Kanpur, and Council of Scientific & Industrial Research (CSIR-IMMT), Bhubaneswar, India for a brief period. During Spring 2010, I worked at IIIT Bhubaneswar, India, as part of my master thesis. You can read more about me using the link here.

To this end, my mission is to utilize my passion in analyzing the implications of rapidly changing technology for socioeconomic inequities at large while serving as an example of the success that will shape the future of our society.

Research Interests

  • Emerging communications and networking technologies (5G/6G, mmWave, Edge/Fog)
  • Internet of Things including IIoT, UAV, V2X
  • Application of AI and ML in wireless
  • Bio-inspired computing & networking
